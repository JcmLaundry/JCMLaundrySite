​About Our Store

At JCM Laundry, we are proud to offer top-quality laundry services to our customers. Our store is equipped with state-of-the-art machines and staffed by a team of experienced professionals who are dedicated to providing the best possible care for your clothes. We take pride in our attention to detail and strive to ensure that every garment that leaves our store is clean, fresh, and perfectly pressed.

In addition to our laundry services, we also offer basic alterations to help make your clothes fit and look their best. We believe that everyone deserves to feel confident in their appearance, and we are dedicated to helping you achieve that. Thank you for choosing JCM Laundry for all of your laundry needs.
